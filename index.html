Biography

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian former international cricketer from India, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He was born on April24,1973,in Mumbai, India

Achievements

Cricket Record

Sachin Tendulkar holds numerous cricket records, including being the highest run-scorer in both Test and One-Day International (ODI) cricket. He achieve several milestones throughtout his illustrious career

International Honour

He received several award and honor, including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 2014. His contribution to India cricket unparalled

Records

Sachin Tendulkar holds records for the most centuries in both Test and ODI formats, and many more. his records are a testament to his exceptional skill and longevity in the sport.
