Memorable Moments

  • 2012 Asia Cup match against Bangladesh: Tendulkar scored 114 runs off 147 deliveries, becoming the first batsman to score 100 international centuries across formats.
  • 2013 Wankhede Stadium farewell: Tendulkar hung up his boots as an international cricketer in an emotional ceremony at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.
  • 1999 World Cup match against Kenya: Tendulkar scored an unbeaten 140 runs, including 16 boundaries and 3 sixes, and was named Man of the Match.
